The Monkey

2025
Directed by Osgood Perkins • Horror • USA

Based on Stephen King's short story, "The Monkey" follows twin brothers who discover their family's toy monkey is connected to a series of gruesome deaths. When the dormant toy reenters their lives decades later, the brothers must overcome their estranged relationship to defeat the monkey's horrifying curse once and for all.

Michael S
February 18, 2025

Perkins delivers another atmospheric slow-burn that masterfully builds dread through a minimalist approach. The cursed toy monkey becomes a genuinely unnerving presence through clever camera work and sound design rather than cheap jump scares. Wood and James have excellent chemistry as estranged brothers forced to confront both supernatural horror and family trauma.

Sarah K
February 15, 2025

While the concept is undeniably creepy (that monkey!), the film struggles with pacing in its middle section. Strong performances from the cast, particularly Tatiana Maslany in a supporting role, help elevate material that occasionally feels stretched thin from its short story origins. The final act, however, delivers some genuinely unsettling imagery that will stick with you.
